YourSight: Accessibility App

YourSight is an AI powered accessibility app I developed and Published. It was made using Flutter and Flask, with MongoDB databases. It is currently published on the appstore! I presented the app at the Middlesex Association for the Blind; I got feedback and conducted end user acceptance testing before publication.

Custom Flutter AR pluggin

While building YourSight it turns out that flutter doesnt have a distance sensing plugin. To remedy this, I built my own native swift method channel which acts as a native plugin.
